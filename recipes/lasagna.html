J. Kenji Lopez-Alt's Lasagna a la Bolognese

Description

This is a traditional lasagna bolognese from the Serious Eats recipe book by J. Kenji Lopez-Alt. The recipe uses three types of meat for a very reach bolognese, and is much better if you make ricotta fresh, from scratch. Follow these links to find recipes for the ricotta and the fresh pasta.

Ingredients

For the Ragú Bolognese:

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2/3 lb ground lamb
  • 2/3 lb ground pork
  • 2/3 lb ground veal
  • 4 ounces chicken livers, finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large carrots, peeled, and cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 3 large celery ribs, peeled, and cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh sage leaves, finely chopped
  • Large pinch red pepper flakes
  • 1 28-ounce can D.O.P. San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups dry red wine
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups homemade chicken or veal stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon Worscestershire sauce or fish sauce
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup minced basil and parsley mix
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Basil

For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 3 cups homemade fresh ricotta cheese or store-bought whole milk cottage cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup minced basil and parsley mixture

For the Besciamella:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 lb dry, whole-milk mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • 1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

For Assembly:

  • Fifteen 4- by 8-inch sheets of fresh rolled pasta, or 15 pieces no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 4 oz parmesan, grated
  • 2 tbsp basil and parsley mixture
