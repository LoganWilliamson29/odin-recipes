J. Kenji Lopez-Alt's Lasagna a la Bolognese

Description

This is a traditional lasagna bolognese from the Serious Eats recipe book by J. Kenji Lopez-Alt

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups Flour
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • 1/2 lb ground lamb
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Basil
